What is App Development?
app development is the process in which developers create an
application to be used on smartphones, tablets and other mobile
devices. While app development sometimes involves creating a
web-based app or a desktop version of the app, the majority of app
development projects are deployed to mobile and tablet devices. There
are many features and considerations that factor into app
development, which will be further explored in this article.
